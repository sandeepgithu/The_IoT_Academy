Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of enrolling on this program?

+

Enrolling in this program offers numerous benefits including: industry-recognized certification from IIT Roorkee, hands-on projects with real-world applications, mentorship from industry experts, 100% placement support, lifetime access to course materials, and networking opportunities with fellow learners and professionals in the field.

What format is the program conducted in?

+

The program is conducted in a live, instructor-led online format. All sessions are interactive and held in real-time, allowing you to ask questions and engage with instructors. Additionally, all classes are recorded and made available for lifetime access, so you can review the content at your convenience.

Where and how are the live classes conducted?

+

Live classes are conducted online through our secure learning platform. You can attend from anywhere with a stable internet connection. Classes feature screen sharing, live coding demonstrations, Q&A sessions, and interactive discussions. All you need is a laptop/desktop and a good internet connection to participate.

What is the process for enrolling on the program?

+

The enrollment process is simple: 1) Fill out the registration form on our website, 2) Our admissions team will contact you to discuss program details, 3) Complete the payment process (flexible payment options available), 4) Receive access to the learning portal and pre-course materials, 5) Join the orientation session before the batch starts.

Are there any prerequisites to enrol in this?

+

Basic knowledge of programming and mathematics is recommended but not mandatory. The program is designed to accommodate learners from various backgrounds. We provide foundational modules to help you get up to speed. A bachelor's degree in any field and enthusiasm to learn are the primary requirements.

If my class gets missed, what can be done?

+

Don't worry if you miss a class! All sessions are recorded and uploaded to the learning portal within 24 hours. You can watch the recording at your convenience. Additionally, you can reach out to our support team or instructors through the discussion forum if you have any questions about the missed content.

What is the process to be followed after enrolment?

+

After enrollment: 1) You'll receive login credentials to access the learning portal, 2) Complete the pre-course preparation modules, 3) Attend the orientation session to meet instructors and fellow students, 4) Follow the course schedule and attend live classes regularly, 5) Complete assignments and projects on time, 6) Utilize placement support services as you progress.

Do I need to maintain any specific attendance or minimum score?

+

Yes, to receive the completion certificate, you need to maintain at least 75% attendance in live sessions and score a minimum of 60% in assignments and projects. However, since all classes are recorded, you can watch them later if you miss a live session. Active participation and timely submission of assignments are encouraged for better learning outcomes.

How many projects will I work on during the program?

+

You'll work on 6-8 hands-on projects throughout the program, including capstone projects. These projects cover various aspects of data science, machine learning, and AI, giving you practical experience with real-world datasets and business problems.

What kind of career support do you provide?

+

We provide comprehensive career support including resume building, interview preparation, mock interviews, portfolio development, job referrals, and direct placement assistance with our 200+ hiring partners. Our dedicated placement team works with you throughout and after the program.

What are the payment options available?

+

We offer multiple payment options including one-time payment, EMI options through our financial partners (Razorpay, Zest), credit/debit card payments, and education loans at 0% interest. You can choose the payment plan that works best for you.

How can I avail the scholarship?

+

Scholarships up to 35% are available based on various criteria including early bird enrollment, academic background, work experience, and referrals. Contact our admissions team to learn about current scholarship opportunities and eligibility criteria. Limited scholarships are available per batch.
